X-treme Rock Paper Scissors

Rules

You have 5 of each weapon type: rock, paper, and scissors. If you play a weapon more than 3 times in a row, that weapon breaks. (It'll break on the fourth use.)

How To Play

Select the weapon on the "User" side that you want to play, and then press your enter or the BATTLE! button.

In the log, the number of times each weapon has been used in consecutive turns is indicated by tally marks.

The log and values are updated as you go to the next round.
