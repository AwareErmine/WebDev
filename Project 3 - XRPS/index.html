X-treme Rock Paper Scissors

Rules

You have 5 of each weapon type: rock, paper, and scissors. Each of these weapons may be used 3 times in a row.

How To Play

Select the weapon on the "User" side that you want to play, and then press enter or the BATTLE! button.

The number of uses left of each weapon is indicated by R or the remainder of each weapon type.
